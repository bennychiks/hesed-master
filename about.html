Our Vision.

To raise men and women who will not walt the faith with clutches. (Heb. 11:32-34).
At Powerlife Christian Faith International, our vision is rooted in Hebrews 11:32-34. We aspire to cultivate individuals whose unwavering faith propels them to triumph over challenges. Join us in nurturing a community where faith isn't just held but lived out boldly. Together, let's embody the resilient spirit of those commended in Scripture.

Our Mission.

To reposition men and women for dominion through the word of God. (Luke 10:19).
Our mission at Powerlife Christian Faith International is anchored in Luke 10:19. We are committed to empowering individuals for dominion through the transformative power of God's Word. Join us in the journey to unlock spiritual authority and purpose. Together, let's live out the promise of dominion through the life-changing teachings of the Scriptures.
